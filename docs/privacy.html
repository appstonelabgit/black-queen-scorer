Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 April 2026

Black Queen Scorer (“the app”, “we”) is developed and maintained by AppStoneLab. This page explains, in plain English, what data the app handles — and, more importantly, what it does not.

Summary

Black Queen Scorer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you enter — player names, scores, session history — is stored only on your device.

Data the app collects about you

None. The app has:

  • No analytics SDK
  • No crash reporting
  • No advertising SDK
  • No telemetry
  • No account system
  • No network requests of any kind

We cannot see your data because we do not receive your data.

Data you create inside the app

When you add players, record rounds, and finish sessions, the app stores that information in your device's private application storage (via Hive, an on-device local database). That data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to other apps
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be deleted at any time from Settings → Data → Delete all history

Permissions

The app requests no runtime permissions. It uses only the private storage every app gets by default.

When you tap Share on the session summary, the app generates an image on your device and hands it to the system share sheet. The app never uploads anything; whatever you do with that image (AirDrop, WhatsApp, email, etc.) is between you and the destination app.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13. There is no sign-up and no account.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will publish the updated version at the same URL and update the "Last updated" date at the top.
