What Black Queen Scorer does

Built for the one friend who always keeps score on card night. Setup takes seconds. Each round is a few taps. The math is transparent and always correct.

  • Sessions in seconds Add 4 to 12 players, tap start. Recent names are remembered for next time.
  • 10-second round entry Pick the bidder, tap teammates, type the bid on a custom keypad, tap Won or Lost.
  • Always-correct math Totals are recomputed from the rounds list on every change. Edit freely — nothing goes stale.
  • Optional bidder bonus A ±bonus that applies only to the bidder. Toggle per session.
  • Live leaderboard Rows reorder smoothly as scores move. Score pulses green on gain, red on loss.

Frequently asked questions

What is Black Queen Scorer?

A mobile score tracker for bidding-based team card games. It replaces the pen-and-paper scoring sheet that one person always ends up maintaining on card nights. The app does not simulate card play — it handles scoring only.

Which card games does it support?

Any team bidding game where one player bids an amount, a team tries to win that bid, and the opposition wins if the team fails. Common examples: Court Piece, Rang (Trump), 29, Partner 29, Black Queen, and many regional variants.

How does the scoring math work?

On a win, the bidder's team gains +bid, the opposition loses -bid. On a loss, the signs reverse. Optionally, the bidder alone gains or loses an additional ±bonus. All totals are recomputed from the rounds list every time they are displayed — so editing or deleting a round always yields correct scores.

Does it work offline?

Gameplay works fully offline — all scoring data is stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only used to load ads.

Does it show ads or collect data?

The app shows ads via Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and related data for ad delivery and measurement under Google's policies. Gameplay data (players, rounds, scores) never leaves your device. No account system. See the privacy policy for detail.

How many players per session?

Between 4 and 12.

Can I edit a round after saving it?

Yes. Tap any round tile to edit the bidder, team, bid, or result. Totals recompute instantly.

Does the app cost money?

No. It's free on the App Store. Supported by ads; no in-app purchases.

What platforms is it on?

iOS 13 and later, Android 5 (API 21) and later.
