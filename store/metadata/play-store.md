## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
The fastest scorer for bidding card games.

Black Queen Scorer tracks scores across rounds for team card games like Court Piece, Rang, 29, and similar bidding variants. Built for the friend who always ends up keeping score — so the game never has to pause.

KEY FEATURES

• Start a session in seconds. Add 4 to 12 players with one tap each. Names from past sessions are remembered for next time.

• Enter a round in under 10 seconds. Pick the bidder, tap teammates, enter the bid on a custom keypad, tap Won or Lost.

• Always-correct math. Scores are recomputed from scratch after every edit or delete — no stale totals to fix.

• Optional bidder bonus. Configure a ±bonus that applies only to the bidder. Fully transparent.

• Live leaderboard. Rows reorder smoothly as scores change. Score pulses green on gain, red on loss.

• Round history. Every round tile shows the bidder, bid, and result. Edit or delete any round — the leaderboard recalculates instantly.

• Celebratory finish. Podium, fun stats (biggest single win, boldest bidder, longest streak), shareable WhatsApp-friendly summary card.

• Lifetime stats. Top winner, top earner, cold streak, most bids won — tracked across every session you play.

• 100% offline. No account, no signup, no cloud, no network calls. Everything stays on your device.

• No ads, no tracking, no data collection. Nothing ever leaves your phone.

WHO IT'S FOR

Groups of 4 to 12 friends playing in person. One person enters scores while everyone else plays.

SUPPORTED GAMES

Any bidding-based team card game where one player bids an amount, a team tries to win that bid, and the opposition wins if the team fails. Works well for Court Piece, Rang (Trump), 29, Partner 29, Black Queen, and many regional variants.

PRIVACY

No analytics, no ads, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no account. Everything is stored locally on your device. You can wipe all data anytime from Settings → Data.

Made for card nights.
```
