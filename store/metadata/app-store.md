**Description** (max 4000 chars):

```
The fastest scorer for bidding card games.

Black Queen Scorer tracks scores across rounds for team card games like Court Piece, Rang, 29, and similar bidding variants. Built for the one friend who always ends up keeping score — so the game never has to pause.

KEY FEATURES

• Start a session in seconds
  Add 4 to 12 players with one tap each. Names from past sessions are remembered.

• Enter a round in under 10 seconds
  Pick the bidder, tap teammates, enter the bid on a custom keypad, tap Won or Lost. Done.

• Always-correct math
  Scores are recomputed from scratch after every edit or delete — no stale totals, no manual fixes.

• Optional bidder bonus
  Configure a ±bonus that applies only to the bidder, fully transparent.

• Live leaderboard
  Rows reorder smoothly as scores change. Score pulses green on gain, red on loss.

• Round history
  See every round played. Edit or delete any round — the leaderboard recalculates instantly.

• Celebratory finish
  Podium, fun stats (biggest single win, boldest bidder, longest streak), shareable WhatsApp-friendly card.

• Lifetime stats
  Top winner, top earner, cold streak, most bids won — tracked across every session you play.

• 100% offline
  No account, no signup, no cloud, no network calls. Everything stays on your device.

• No ads, no tracking, no data collection
  Nothing ever leaves your phone.

WHO IT'S FOR

Groups of 4 to 12 friends playing in person. One person enters scores on their phone while everyone else plays.

SUPPORTED GAMES

Any bidding-based team card game where one player bids an amount, a team (the bidder plus optional teammates) tries to win that bid, and opposition wins if the team fails. This covers Court Piece, Rang (Trump), 29, Partner 29, Black Queen, and many regional variants.

PRIVACY

No analytics, no ads, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no account. Everything is stored locally in your device's private app storage. You can wipe it all at any time from Settings → Data → Delete all history.

Made for card nights.
```
